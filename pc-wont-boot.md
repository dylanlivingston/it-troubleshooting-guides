# PC Will Not Boot

## Initial checks
- Verify the PC has power
- Check that cables are firmly connected
- Listen for fans or beep codes

## Common causes
- Loose power cable
- Faulty power supply
- Hardware failure
- Corrupt boot device

## Troubleshooting steps
1. Power off and unplug the PC
2. Reseat power and display cables
3. Disconnect non-essential peripherals
4. Attempt to boot again
5. If available, test with a known-good power cable or outlet
